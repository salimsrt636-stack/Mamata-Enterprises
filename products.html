❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Which pump is best for my borewell?

+

The best pump depends on your borewell depth, water requirement, and power availability. For depths up to 70 meters, we recommend submersible pumps. For deeper borewells (100-200 meters), borewell pumps are ideal. Our technical team can help you choose the right pump after assessing your requirements.

What is the warranty period for pumps?

+

Most of our pumps come with a 1-2 year warranty on the motor and pump assembly. Solar pumps have a 3-year warranty on the pump and 5 years on solar panels. Warranty covers manufacturing defects and performance issues.

Do you provide installation services?

+

Yes, we provide complete installation services by certified technicians. Our installation package includes site assessment, pump installation, electrical connections, testing, and 30-day post-installation support.

Are solar pumps worth the investment?

+

Absolutely! Solar pumps eliminate electricity costs, are eco-friendly, and have a long lifespan (20+ years). With government subsidies available, the return on investment is excellent. Plus, they provide reliable water supply even during power outages.

What maintenance is required for the pumps?

+

Regular maintenance includes annual servicing, checking electrical connections, and cleaning filters. We offer annual maintenance packages that include inspection, cleaning, and minor repairs. Most pumps require minimal maintenance when properly installed.
